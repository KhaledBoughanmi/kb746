Khaled Boughanmi

Khaled Boughanmi is an Assistant Professor at the Johnson School of Management at Cornell University, where is he is a member of the of Marketing & Management Communication division. He is also a member of FinTech at Cornell.

His primary research interests are in the area of data-driven decision making, Bayesian nonparametrics and machine learning with a focus on applications in e-commerce, personalization, online marketing and service systems.

He teaches core MBA courses in marketing management. He is a recipient of the Core Faculty Award at SC Johsnon Graduate School of Management.

Khaled is a graduate of Louis Le Grand (France) with a major in mathematics and physics and ENSAE ParisTech (France), Ecole National de la Statistique et de l'Administration Economique, with a major in applied mathematics, statistics and economics. He also received a M.Sc. and a Ph.D. from Columbia University.
